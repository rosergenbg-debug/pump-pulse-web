Privacy & legal information

Research tool — not financial advice

PUMP Pulse provides mathematical research signals only. It does not execute orders, manage money or guarantee results. Cryptoassets can lose their entire value.

Local storage and cookies

Language, strategy settings, fees, cookie choice and your manual position are stored only in this browser. The optional first-party visitor cookie lasts up to one year and prevents duplicate counting. No advertising or third-party analytics cookies are used.

Private messages

If you send a message, the supplied name, email, message, language and time are stored privately so the site operator can answer. Other visitors cannot read them. You may request access, correction or deletion through the same contact form.

Purpose, legal basis and retention

Optional counting and contact messages are processed with your consent. Necessary browser settings serve the functions you request; security logs serve safe delivery. Messages remain until answered, deleted by the operator or deletion is requested.

Hosting and technical data

The hosting provider may process technical request data such as IP address, time and requested URL and may set a strictly necessary security cookie such as __cf_bm. Market data is requested server-side; your browser is not connected directly to market-data providers.

Your rights

You may withdraw consent and request access, correction, deletion, restriction or objection through the contact form. You may also complain to your competent data-protection authority.

Operator contact

Contact the operator through the private message form. This experimental page is free and does not sell products or execute transactions.
